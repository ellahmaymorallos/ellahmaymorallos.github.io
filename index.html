Chug Fries & Donut Babies

Chug Fries and Donut Babies was opened in 2023, it is an online store limited for fries with drinks and mini donuts. It was started in an online shop created by the two founders, Ellah May and Mary Joy. The brand is known for its quality and tasty food served with different flavors of fresh drinks each day. They go beyond serving mini donuts first and recently added fries with drinks for new products to see in the menu. In the same year Chug Fries and Donut Babies built a mini cart to accommodate customers on streets while still having their online platforms as another source of their clients. Their commitment not just to their customers but also to their business itself to always serve nothing but the best quality product with mind blowing taste that makes them unique with other brands. Every donut they bake and fries they make shows their dedication in keeping the essence of their vision and mission for their brand.
